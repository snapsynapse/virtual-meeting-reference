    Features

    Integrated AI Note-Taker
    management 7 platforms
    Built-in AI capability that automatically generates meeting summaries, action items, key topics, and follow-up tasks from live or recorded meetings without requiring a third-party bot.
    Breakout Rooms
    management 16 platforms
    Ability to split a meeting into smaller sub-sessions for group discussions or activities, with host controls for assignment and timing.
    Chat and Messaging
    communication 22 platforms
    Persistent and in-meeting text communication including direct messages, group channels, and threaded conversations.
    Polling and Q&A
    engagement 16 platforms
    Interactive tools for collecting real-time participant input through polls, surveys, quizzes, and structured Q&A sessions.
    Recording and Transcription
    management 21 platforms
    Capability to record meetings (locally or to cloud) and generate transcripts or captions for review and accessibility.
    Screen Sharing
    collaboration 22 platforms
    Ability to share a full desktop, specific application window, or browser tab with meeting participants in real time.
    Video Conferencing
    communication 22 platforms
    Core capability to host real-time video meetings with multiple participants, including gallery and speaker views.
    Virtual Backgrounds
    engagement 18 platforms
    Visual effects that replace or blur a participant's real background with images, videos, or immersive scenes during video calls.
    Whiteboard
    collaboration 13 platforms
    Shared digital canvas for real-time collaborative drawing, diagramming, and brainstorming during or outside of meetings.
