## Summary

Persistent and in-meeting text communication including direct messages, group channels, and threaded conversations.

## What Counts

- In-meeting chat with public and private messaging
- Persistent team channels outside of meetings
- File sharing within chat
- Threaded conversations and message reactions

## What Does Not Count

- Email-based communication
- SMS or phone-based text messaging
