## Summary

Interactive tools for collecting real-time participant input through polls, surveys, quizzes, and structured Q&A sessions.

## What Counts

- Live polls with multiple question types (multiple choice, rating, open-ended)
- Structured Q&A with upvoting and moderation
- Quiz functionality with scoring
- Results shared live or after the session

## What Does Not Count

- Informal hand-raising without structured collection
- Post-meeting surveys sent via email
