## Summary

Built-in AI capability that automatically generates meeting summaries, action items, key topics, and follow-up tasks from live or recorded meetings without requiring a third-party bot.

## What Counts

- Native AI-generated meeting summaries and recaps
- Automatic action item extraction
- Smart chapter and topic detection in recordings
- AI-powered follow-up task suggestions
- Real-time note generation during the meeting

## What Does Not Count

- Third-party note-taking bots (Otter.ai, Fireflies, etc.) joining via integration
- Manual note-taking or shared documents
- Basic transcription without AI summarization
- Recording-only without intelligent processing
