## Summary

Core capability to host real-time video meetings with multiple participants, including gallery and speaker views.

## What Counts

- Multi-party video with configurable layouts (gallery, speaker, sidebar)
- HD and full-HD video quality options
- Virtual background and background blur
- Video pinning and spotlight

## What Does Not Count

- Audio-only calling without video capability
- Pre-recorded video playback without live interaction
