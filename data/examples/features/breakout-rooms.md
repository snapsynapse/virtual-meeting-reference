## Summary

Ability to split a meeting into smaller sub-sessions for group discussions or activities, with host controls for assignment and timing.

## What Counts

- Automatic or manual participant assignment
- Host broadcast messaging to all rooms
- Timer-based auto-close of breakout sessions
- Participants can ask for host help from within a room

## What Does Not Count

- Scheduling separate meetings for sub-groups
- Using separate meeting links for breakout discussions
