## Summary

Visual effects that replace or blur a participant's real background with images, videos, or immersive scenes during video calls.

## What Counts

- Static image backgrounds
- Background blur and soft focus
- Video or animated backgrounds
- Branded or organization-supplied background libraries

## What Does Not Count

- Physical green screens without software support
- Camera filters that modify facial appearance rather than background
