## Summary

Shared digital canvas for real-time collaborative drawing, diagramming, and brainstorming during or outside of meetings.

## What Counts

- Real-time multi-user drawing and annotation
- Sticky notes, shapes, and text tools
- Persistent boards saved for later access
- Templates for common brainstorming activities

## What Does Not Count

- Static image annotation without real-time collaboration
- Annotation overlays on shared screens (that is screen sharing annotation)
