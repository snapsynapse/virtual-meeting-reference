## Summary

Capability to record meetings (locally or to cloud) and generate transcripts or captions for review and accessibility.

## What Counts

- Cloud-based and local recording options
- Automatic transcription of recorded meetings
- Live closed captions during meetings
- Searchable transcript archives

## What Does Not Count

- Third-party screen recording tools used independently
- Manual note-taking without automated capture
