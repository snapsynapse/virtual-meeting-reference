## Timeline

| Milestone | Date | Notes |
|-----------|------|-------|
| Launch | 2012-11-01 | Launched by Switch Communications as a no-PIN conference calling service |
| Acquired by Dialpad | 2015-07-01 | Dialpad acquired Switch Communications and UberConference |
| Video conferencing added | 2017-01-01 | Expanded from audio-only to include video conferencing |
| Rebranded to Dialpad Meetings | 2020-01-01 | UberConference rebranded as Dialpad Meetings |
| Discontinued | 2020-06-01 | UberConference brand fully retired |
